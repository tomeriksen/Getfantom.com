Quick start guide

Turning ON Fanband

  1. Click and hold the button on your band for 1 second.
  2. The band will vibrate and turn ON.

Connecting to the App

  1. Ensure your Fantom is switched on, and that your mobile phone’s Bluetooth is switched on.
  2. Start the Fantom App and follow the on screen instructions to pair and connect your Fantom device.
  3. Unique ID will show on the Fantom as “Fantom XXXX”. Search for this ID within the Fantom app.
  4. You must allow location and accept the “Pair” option within the App to complete connection.
  5. To ensure delivery of content to your Fantom, keep phone charged, turn off low power mode and never force close the Fantom App.

Turning OFF Fanband

  1. When the Fantom is powered on, hold the button for 3 seconds to turn the Fantom OFF.
  2. The band will vibrate and show “Powering off..” as it shuts down.

Using the Band

  1. Lift the Fantom device towards you to view content, or click the button.
  2. When new content is received, the Fantom will vibrate. Once the message is viewed, Fantom will resume to showing time.

Troubleshooting

How do I check that my Fantom is connected?

  1. Within the Fantom App, you should see the connected device's battery percentage level.
  2. If you see Fanband Disconnected. This means that the Fantom has lost connection.

I cannot see my Fantom XXXX ID inside the App

  1. Go to the Bluetooth Settings in your phone and check that the device is not already connected.
  2. If you see Accessory or a long code like XX:XX:XX:XX:XX:XX then tap to see if the Fantom ID reveals itself. Do not pair from this menu, but instead go into the Fantom App and search again.
  3. You should now see the band listed.

How can I reconnect the Fantom from within the App?

  1. From the Fantom App settings page, tap the bottom right icon, and select Logout.
  2. Go to Bluetooth Settings on your phone and tap Forget device.
  3. Factory reset the Fanband as shown in the video above.

I still can't see the Fantom device, or I think it might be paired to another mobile phone

  1. Power on the band
  2. When you see Pair your phone to Fantom XXXX, swipe left on your Fantom and hold down Factory reset.
  3. After this you can search for the Fantom device from a new phone.
  4. If you can't get to this option, then the existing Bluetooth connected phone is likely still in range. You will need to move out of range, or turn off Bluetooth before being able to select Factory reset.

The vibration seems very weak

  1. Power off the Fantom and then power it on again quickly.
  2. The vibration should feel stronger from now on.

The Fantom won’t charge or respond at all

  1. Hold the button for 15 seconds and power on again.
